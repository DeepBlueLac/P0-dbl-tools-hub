# DBL-TOOLS Design System

## Design Read

Marketing and product index for international tool users. The visual language is dark technical editorial with a precise object photographed like a studio artifact.

## Dials

- `DESIGN_VARIANCE`: 8. Asymmetric split hero and mixed editorial rows.
- `MOTION_INTENSITY`: 6. Motion communicates entry hierarchy and product discovery.
- `VISUAL_DENSITY`: 3. Airy, few sections, no decorative card grid.

## Tokens

- Surface: ink charcoal `#090c0d`, elevated charcoal `#111517`.
- Text: warm off-white `#f4f0e9`, muted stone `#a7aaa6`.
- Accent: coral orange `#ff765f`. One accent across the page.
- Radius: 6px for controls and media frames. Pills are reserved for status only.
- Type: Space Grotesk for display, Manrope for interface text.

## Visual rules

- The hero is a single composition: brand, one headline, one sentence, one CTA group, one generated image.
- Product cards are not a repeated 3-column grid. Products use one featured row and a sparse index.
- Motion is limited to entrance reveals and hover feedback. `prefers-reduced-motion` removes motion.
- The generated image is an original project-bound asset at `public/media/dbl-tools-hero.png`.
- No fake metrics, fake testimonials, decorative emoji, or invented customer logos.
